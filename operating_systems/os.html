About Me

Race Condition

The images show that thread race conditions during bank transactions cause inconsistent and unpredictable intermediate balances due to the simultaneous execution of deposits and withdrawals. Despite these inconsistencies, the final balance is correct in both cases, as the sum of transactions balances out. However, this highlights the critical need for synchronization in concurrent programming to ensure consistent states throughout the process.
